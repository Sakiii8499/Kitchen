Milkshake

Ingredients

4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream, 1/4 c. milk, Whipped topping, for garnish, Sprinkles, for garnish, Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Method

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
