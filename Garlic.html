Garlic bread

Ingredients

Garlic powder 1 tablespoon, Garlic chopped as required, Refined flour (maida) 1 cup + for dusting, Fresh yeast 2 teaspoons, Castor sugar 1 1/2 teaspoons, Salt to taste, Olive oil 2 tablespo for greasing, Dried rosemary 2 teaspoons, Dried basil 2 teaspoons, Black peppercorns crushed 2 teaspoons, Dried oregano 2 teaspoons

Method

  1. Take yeast in a small bowl. Add ½ teaspoon castor sugar and 2-3 tablespoons warm water, mix and set aside for 5 minutes to activate.
  2. Sift flour into a mixing bowl. Add salt and remaining castor sugar and mix.
  3. Preheat oven to 180º C. Mix together rosemary, basil, crushed peppercorns, garlic powder, oregano and paprika in another small bowl.
  4. Grate mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Grease a baking tray with some olive oil, place stuffed garlic bread on it.
  6. Take it out of the oven, place on a serving platter, brush the top with some melted butter, cut into wedges and serve hot with tomato ketchup.
