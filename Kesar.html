Kesar Badam Ice-cream

Ingredients

1 cup heavy cream, 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk, 10-12 strands of saffron with 1 tbsp milk (soak the saffron with milk), 2-3 drops yellow food color(optional), some chopped almonds

Method

  1. First of all take a blender and add 1 cup chilled heavy cream. Make sure cream should be chilled. Then add 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk along with 10-12 soaked saffron and at last goes in 2-3 drops of yellow food color.
  2. Now blend it for 2-3 minutes. Check the consistency in between, you need to blend it until it becomes smooth and thick.
  3. Now take one air tight container and spread some chopped nuts and add the blended cream. Mix everything make sure you don't over mix else texture might become thin.
  4. At last spread some more almond on top of it for divine look and cover it with cling wrap from all the sides so it wont be crystallized.
  5. Cover the lid of container and freeze it for 8-10 hours or until it sets.
  6. Once this is set, take it out and after couple of minutes scoop it and serve chilled. Enjoy!!
