Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

4 medium mashed,boiled potato, 2 medium chopped onion, 1/2 cup chopped cauliflower, 1 cup shelled,blanched peas, 3 tablespoon refined oil, salt as required, 8 pav, 2 chopped green chilli, 25 gm melted butter, 1 medium lemon wedges, 2 teaspoon pav bhaji masala

Method

  3. Add tomatoes, salt and cook on medium heat for three to four minutes.
  4. Add mashed peas, cauliflower, potatoes and 2 cups of water. Bring it to a boil and simmer for ten minutes.
  5. Add mashed peas, cauliflower, potatoes and 2 cups of water. Bring it to a boil and simmer for ten minutes.
  6. Garnish the bhaji with chopped coriander leaves, remaining butter and serve hot with pavbhaji.
