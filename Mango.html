Mango Ice-cream

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups peeled and chopped ripe alphonso mangoes, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup condensed milk, 2 cups milk

Method

  1. Combine the mangoes and the sugar and blend till smooth.
  2. Combine the mango pupl and all the remaining ingredients in a deep bowl and mix very well using a whisk.
  3. Pour the mixture into a shallow aluminium container.
  4. Transfer the mixture into the same aluminium container. Cover with an aluminium foil and freeze for 10 hours.
  5. Scoop and serve imeediately.
