Upama

Ingredients

1/2 tsp Ghee, 1/2 cup Sooji / Semolina, 1 tbsp Oil, Raw peanuts, 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds, 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds, 1/4 tsp Hing, Curry leaves, Finely chopped Green chillies, 1/2 cup Finely chopped Onion, 2 tsp Chopped Green beans, Finely chopped Coriander leaves

Method

  1. Heat up a pan on medium heat. Add ghee and sooji. Mix well and roast sooji on medium heat for about 4-5 minutes.
  2. Heat up oil in a pan. Add raw peanuts and fry these change their color.
  3. When peanuts turn light brown, take these out into a dish. In the same oil add mustard seeds and let them pop up.
  4. You can use carrot, green peas instead if bell peppers. You can use veggies of your choice. Mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes more. Add roasted sooji.
  5. Add hot water and mix well. Add salt and mix well. Cover and cook on low to medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  6. The umpa is nice and soft but not sticky. Garnish with coriander leaves and fried peanuts.
