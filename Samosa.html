Samosa

Ingredients

2 cup all purpose flour (maida), 1/4 cup oil, 1 tsp carom seeds, Salt to taste, Water as needed, 4 medium size boiled potato,1/2 cup green peas (fresh or frozen), 2 green chilli chopped

Method

  1. Take maida, carom seeds, salt, and oil (or ghee) in a wide-mouthed bowl.
  2. Mix well and with your fingertips rub the ghee/oil in the flour to get a breadcrumb-like consistency.
  3. Now, add water in multiple small quantities and knead firm dough (harder than paratha dough).
  4. Cover it with wet muslin cloth/any cloth and let it set for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Heat cooking oil in kadai over medium flame.Add cumin seeds and crackle them.
  6. Take one dough ball and press it between your palms to flatten it a little. Put it over rolling board and roll it out into round shaped puri having approx 5-6 inch diameter.
  7. Cut with a knife or a pastry cutter through the center of the samosa sheet into two semi-circles.
  8. When the oil is medium hot, gently slide 3-4 prepared samosa (depending on the size of a pan) in it and quickly reduce the flame to low. Serve samosa with chutney.
