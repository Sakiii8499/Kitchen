Puran poli

Ingredients

Toor dal 1 cup, Organic jaggery chopped 1/2 cup, Green cardamom powder 1/2 teaspoon, Ghee 1 tablespoon, Ghee as required, Whole wheat flour dough as required, Whole wheat flour (atta) for dusting, Osaman to serve

Method

  1. Take toor dal in a large bowl, add sufficient water and wash it 2-3 times.
  2. Transfer the dal into a pressure cooker and add sufficient water, cover and cook under pressure till 3-4 whistles are released. Allow the pressure to reduce completely. Transfer into a bowl and let it cool down to room temperature.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan, add the cooked dal and jaggery and keep mixing it till the mixture comes together and jaggery melts completely.
  4. Heat a non-stick tawa. Divide the dough into equal portions and shape them into balls. Roll each ball in dry flour and roll out into a small disc.
  5. Further roll it into small disc and place it on the hot tawa, cook for 1-2 minutes on each side. Drizzle ghee on both sides and cook till golden brown and crisp.
