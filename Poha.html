Poha

Ingredients

2 cups thick beaten rice (jada poha), 1/2 cup raw peanuts, 2 tbsp oil, 10 curry leaves (kadi patta), 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera), 1/2 tsp mustard seeds ( rai / sarson), 3/4 cup finely chopped onions, 1 1/2 tbsp finely chopped green chillies, 1/2 cup boiled potato cubes, 3/4 tsp turmeric powder (haldi), salt to taste, 1/4 cup finely chopped coriander (dhania)

Method

  1. Place the poha in a strainer, toss well to drain out all excess water. Keep aside.
  2. In a nonstick deep pan, heat the oil in a deep non-stick pan, add the curry leaves, peanuts, cumin seeds and mustard seeds and sauté on a medium flame for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the onions and sauté on a medium flame for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the green chillies and potato and sauté on a medium flame for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the turmeric powder and salt. You can use sugar if you wish to, but it’s completely optional and sauté on a medium flame for 1 minute.
  6. Add the soaked poha and cook on a medium flame for 2 minutes.
  7. Serve the maharashtrian style batata poha hot with a lemon wedge.
