Apple Pie

Ingredients

Apples 1 cup, Refined Flour 6 measures, Chilled butter 60 grams, For filling, Apples peeled and sliced 4 medium, Cinnamon powder 1/2 teaspoon, Sugar Free 10 measure

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F/Gas Mark 6. Sift the flour and Sugar Free into a mixing bowl. Add diced butter to flour and rub in with fingertips till the mixture resembles coarse breadcrumbs.
  2. Sprinkle five tablespoons of chilled water and mix to make a dough without kneading much. Divide the dough into two equal portions and shape each into a ball.
  3. Roll out one portion on a lightly floured surface to a disc of about twelve inches in diameter. Use this to line a nine-inch pie dish, easing the dough in carefully so that it does not get stretched.
  4. Trim off the excess dough. Roll out the remaining dough to a rectangle of one-eighth inch thickness. With a sharp knife, cut out half inch strips.
  5. Cover it with dough strips in a crisscross pattern and bake in the preheated oven for forty-five minutes.
  6. Let the pie cool in the dish. Cut into wedges and serve.
