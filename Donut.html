Donuts

Ingredients

Refined flour 2 tablespoons+ 1 cup, Fresh yeast 1/2 tablespoon, Powdered sugar 2 tablespoons, Milk 1/4 cup, Butter 2 tablespoons, Baking powder 1/2 teaspoon, Salt a pinch, Melted dark chocolate as required, Colourful sprinklers for garnish

Method

  1. Take fresh yeast in a small bowl, add ½ tbsp powdered sugar and ¼ cup warm water and mix well. Set aside to activate.
  2. Heat milk in a non-stick pan, remaining powdered sugar and butter and let it melt. Take it off the heat.
  3. Take refined flour in a large bowl, add activated yeast, baking powder, salt and milk-butter mixture, mix well and knead into soft dough.
  4. Cover with a damp muslin cloth and set aside for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Dip half the donuts in melted white chocolate and the remaining in melted dark chocolate.
  6. Once the chocolate sets, place them on a serving platter, garnish with colourful sprinkles and serve.
