Pineapple Juice

Ingredients

2 to 2.5 cups chopped pineapple or 1 small pineapple chopped, 1 to 1.25 cups water or add as required, 6 to 8 ice cubes, 2 to 3 tablespoon sugar or add as required, ⅛ teaspoon black salt (optional), ½ teaspoon roasted cumin powder (optional)

Method

  1. First step is to peel-chop or chop-peel the pineapples.
  2. Take the chopped pineapple in a blender jar.
  3. Add 2 to 3 tbsp sugar and 6 to 8 ice cubes. Also add 1 cup water.
  4. Blend till smooth. For a thin consistency, you can add some more water.
  5. Serve pineapple juice garnished with pineapple wedges or slices.
