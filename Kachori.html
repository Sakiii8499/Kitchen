Shegaon Kachori

Ingredients

2 cups maida, pinch of salt, 1/4 cup Oil, 1 little less than 1/2 cup water, 1 tbsp cumin seeds, 1 tbsp fennel seeds, 1 tbsp coriander seeds, oil, chat masala, a pinch of sugar, water, oil for frying

Method

  1. Take maida into a dish. Add salt, oil and mix everything well together. The mixture should come together nicely.
  2. Heat up a pan on medium heat. Add cumin seeds, fennel seeds, coriander seeds, and roast on medium heat a little until these release aroma.
  3. Add ginger-garlic-green chili paste and fry for about a minute. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder and fry for about 30 seconds. Add besan and roast well.
  4. Take a small portion of dough and make it smooth and even. Make pari for it as we make for modak.
  5. Fill the stuffing and close it up. Roll it into the shape of kachori.
  6. Sprinkle the oil on kachori until it floats on the oil.
  7. Yummy Shegaon kachori is all-ready.
