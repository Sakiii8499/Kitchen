Pizza

Ingredients

Dough, Cornmeal 3 tablespoons, Refined flour 2 tablespo for dusting, Turmeric powder 1/4 teaspoon, Carom seeds a pinch, Salt to taste, Red chilli powder 1/2 teaspoon, Oil 2 tablespoons, Stuffing, Red kidney beans (rajma) boiled 1 cup, Mozzarella cheese grated 1/4 cup

Method

  1. To prepare the dough, take cornmeal in a bowl, add refined flour, turmeric powder, carom seeds, salt, red chilli powder and 1 tbsp oil in a bowl.
  2. Divide dough into 2 equal portions and shape them into balls.
  3. Heat a shallow non-stick pan, place one roti at a time in it, apply some butter on it and flip. Apply some butter on the other side too and cook, turning sides till light golden brown specs appear on both sides.
  4. Spread a thick layer of stuffing on one roti, place the other roti on top. Apply pizza sauce evenly on top and top it with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat till base become hard and the cheese melts.
  6. Transfer pizza on the worktop and cut into wedges with a pizza slicer.
