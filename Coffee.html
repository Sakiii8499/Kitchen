Coffee

Ingredients

1.5 teaspoons nescafe instant coffee, 1.5 tablespoons sugar or add as per taste, 3 tablespoons hot water, 1 cup milk (250 ml)

Method

  1. In a cup add instant coffee and sugar. Add 3 tablespoons hot boiling water.
  2. Then take 1 cup milk in a small but heavy sauce pan and on a medium to medium-high flame boil it. When the milk comes to boil pour the milk in a glass jar or a thermos flask.
  3. Shake the jar vigorously & pour the foamy milk in the cup containing coffee.
  4. After adding ⅓ or ½ of the milk to the coffee, stir with a spoon. Then pour remaining milk along with the frothy layer.
  5. Serve cafe style coffee straightway.
