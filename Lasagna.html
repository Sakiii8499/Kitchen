Lasagna

Ingredients

Paneer (cottage cheese) 300 grams, Lasagna sheets 6, Oil 1½ teaspoons, Cumin seeds 1 teaspoon, Green chillies chopped 2, Onions chopped 2 medium

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180ºC. To make paneer bhurji, heat 1 teaspoon oil in a non-stick wok. Add cumin seeds and sauté till the seeds change colour.
  2. Add crumbled cottage cheese and mix well. Add salt, mix well and cook for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Add onion paste and sauté for 2 minutes.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, mix well and sauté well. Add tomato puree and mix well. Increase heat, add 1½ cups water, stir to mix and cook on medium heat for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Add butter and keep mixing. Cook further. Dry roast dried fenugreek leaves till fragrant. Add remaining red chilli powder, remaining garam masala powder and salt to the pan and mix well. Cook further.
  6. Serve hot garnished with a coriander sprig.
