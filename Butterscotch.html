Butterscotch Ice-cream

Ingredients

2 cup heavy cream, 2 teaspoon cashews, 3 tablespoon milk powder, 1 teaspoon butterscotch essence, 500 ml condensed milk, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 teaspoon almonds, edible food color as required

Method

  1. Take a pan over medium flame. To it add sugar. Once the sugar melts (caramelises), add to it chopped almonds, chopped cashews. Coat the nuts well with the sugar. Once done, turn off fame and keep stirring. Transfer the mixture into a dish and let it cool.
  2. Next, in a large bowl add condensed milk, milk powder and butterscotch essence. Mix everything together.
  3. Pour this mixture in the container that will be used for freezing. Put the mixture in the freezer for 1-2 hours.
  4. Now take the prepared praline and break it into pieces. Process in food processor till you get coarse pieces and a fine powder.
  5. Freeze the ice cream again for 6-8 hours, or preferably overnight. Your favourite butterscotch ice cream is now ready to serve.
