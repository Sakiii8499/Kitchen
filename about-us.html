About me

Welcome to Sakshi's Kitchen, an Indian food blog on simple vegetarian recipes. I am Sakshi Chede currently living in Pulgaon. I am currently pursuing Btech. in Mechanical from Government College of Engineering Amravati.

About the website

Coming from a very traditional Indian family, I learnt cooking food from my sweet mother. I started this blog to share my recipes that are simple, quick, healthy and delicious. I share only recipes that are tried, tasted and cherised by my family. What you see on the blog is what I make for my loved ones. Whether you are a newbie or an experienced cook, I am sure you will find exciting and practically possible recipes here that will definitely enhance your cooking skills.
