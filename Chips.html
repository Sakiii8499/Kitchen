Potato Chips

Ingredients

2 potatoes, 6-7 cups of water for immersing potatoes slices, Oil to fry

Method

  1. Take big, round potatoes to make potato chips. Peel the skin of potatoes.
  2. Cut the potatoes into thin slices with the help of chips cutter or any food processor.
  3. Transfer the sliced potatoes into ice cold water and leave it for half an hour.
  4. Take them out of the water and wash them with fresh water. Wash with running water then only if there is too much starch otherwise it is not needed.
  5. Heat sufficient vegetable oil in a wide and deep skillet. When the oil is little smoky hot, medium the flame.
  6. Fry the potato chips till crispy or slight golden spots start to appear on the very edge of the chips.
  7. Take them out of the kadai once the chips get crispy.
  8. Serve the fresh potato chips right away or store them in an airtight container and serve it later with tea or coffee in the evening snack.
