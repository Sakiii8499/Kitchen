Dosa

Ingredients

2 cup parboiled rice, 1/2 cup urad dal, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/4 cup refined oil, 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds, 1/2 kilograms boiled potato, 2 medium chopped green chilli

Method

  1. To prepare the batter for Masala Dosa, wash and soak the rice (with fenugreek added into it) and urad dal in separate containers for approximately 6-8 hours.
  2. Once the rice and urad dal are soaked well, grind them separately in a mixer using the water in which they were soaked, till the mixture reaches a smooth consistency.
  3. Mix the batter of both the ingredients in a bigger container and add salt to it. Combine well and allow it to ferment overnight.
  4. Pour water gradually into the mixture and allow the potatoes to simmer for around 4 minutes. When the mixture is in semi-thick state, turn off the gas and let it rest for a few seconds.
  5. When the colour of dosa's edges turns into brown, reduce the flame and sprinkle few drops of oil on the dosa sides and put 2 tablespoons of filling.
  6. Fold the dosa. Serve hot Masala Dosa with coconut chutney and sambhar.
