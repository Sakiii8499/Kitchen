Vada Pav

Ingredients

1 tbsp oil, 1/2 tbsp finely chopped garlic , 1/2 tbsp finely chopped ginger , 1/2 tbsp finely chopped green chillies, 5 to 7 curry leaves , 1 1/2 cups boiled , peeled and mashed potatoes, oil for deep-frying, 1/2 cup besan , a pinch of turmeric powder , salt to taste

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft, peel them and set them aside.
  2. Now add the boiled potatoes and remove pan from heat. Add turmeric, salt and mash the potatoes using a potato masher. Add chopped cilantro and mix well.
  3. Now make lemon sized balls out of the potato mixture.
  4. To make the batter, take besan in a large bowl. Add turmeric, salt and pinch of baking soda to it. Start adding water, little by little to the bowl.
  5. Heat oil in a kadai/wok on medium-high heat. Dip each potato round into the batter, coat it from all sides. Then drop it carefully into the hot oil.
  6. Fry the vada in hot oil until golden brown in color.
  7. Place the fried vada in the center. Serve the vada pav immediately!
