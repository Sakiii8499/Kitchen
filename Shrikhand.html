Shrikhand puri

Ingredients

4 cups yogurt, 2 cups sugar, 7-8 saffron strands(kesar), 1 tbsp milk, 2 tbsp almonds, pistachios, chopped, 1/2 tsp green cardamom powder, 2 cups wheat flour, 1 tbsp oil, Water as required, Oil to deep fry, Salt to taste

Method

  1. Hang yogurt in a muslin cloth, over night or all liquid from yogurt has drained off.
  2. In a big bowl, add yogurt, sugar & mix well.Dissolve saffron in warm milk & keep aside.
  3. Put thick yogurt over muslin cloth, in small quantities. Rub mixture firmly over cloth, till whole yogurt is sieved.
  4. Now add dissolved saffron, chopped almonds, pistachios, cashew nut & cardamom powder in the yogurt. Mix it well, refrigerate till cold.
  5. Garnish with saffron, almonds, cashew nut.
  6. Divide dough in small sized balls & roll out to make puris.
  7. Heat oil in a pan, deep fry puris from both sides till light brown.
  8. Chilled delicious, creamy shrikhand & hot crispy puri is ready to serve.
