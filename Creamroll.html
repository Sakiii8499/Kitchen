Cream roll

Ingredients

puff pastry sheet as required, 8 tablespoon milk, 2 cup unsalted butter, 2 cup powdered sugar, 2 teaspoon vanilla essence

Method

  1. To start with, take a puff pastry sheet and cut it into a long ribbon-like piece. Cut as many pieces you want. Make sure that all the pieces are neither too wide nor too thin in width.
