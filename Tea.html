Tea

Ingredients

1 tbsp tea powder , 3/4 cup milk , 1 1/2 tbsp sugar

Method

  1. To make indian tea, combine all the ingredients along with 1Â½ cups of water in a non-stick saucepan and boil on a medium flame.
  2. When the mixture boils till the brim, reduce the flame to slow to prevent it from spilling out and continue to boil for 3-4 minutes.
