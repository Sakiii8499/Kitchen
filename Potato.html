Potato Vada

Ingredients

3 boiled,mashed,peeled potato, 1/2 cup rice flour, 2 medium finely chopped green chilli, 1 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1 cup refined oil, 1 cup gram flour (besan), 1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger, 1 cup finely chopped onion, 1 tablespoon juice of 1 lemon, 1 teaspoon salt

Method

  1. To prepare this easy Potato vada recipe, boil, peel and mash the potatoes. Then heat little oil in a pan over medium flame.
  2. Once the oil is sufficiently hot, add onions in it. Saute the onions till they turn slightly pinkish in hue.
  3. Bring the flame to medium-high and keep stirring the mixture.
  4. Then, mix the rice flour and gram flour in a glass bowl.
  5. Once the potato filling is cool enough to handle, take portions of the potato mixture and make small balls of it.
  6. Deep fry the bondas until golden brown. Once done, remove and drain the excess oil.
  7. Serve this easy potato snack recipe with chutney or sauce.
