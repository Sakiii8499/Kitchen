Pasta

Ingredients

Cheese sauce, Processed cheese grated 1/2 cup, Butter 1 tablespoon, Refined flour 1 tablespoon, Milk 3/4 cup, Black peppercorns crushed to taste, Salt to taste,

Method

  1. Heat butter in a non-stick pan, add refined flour and whisk well. Add milk and whisk to avoid lumps.
  2. When the mixture thickens slightly, add cheese and mix till cheese dissolves. Add salt and crushed black pepper and mix well. Take the pan off the heat and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in another non-stick pan, add garlic and saute till raw smell disappears.
  4. Add salt and mix well. Add ketchup, mix well and cook till the mixture is thick. Add crushed black peppercorns and mix well. Take the pan off the heat.
  5. Add pasta and mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes. Grate parmesan cheese into the pan and sprinkle red chilli flakes and mix well.
  6. Transfer into a serving bowl, garnish with parmesan shavings and sprinkle crushed black peppercorns on top and serve hot.
