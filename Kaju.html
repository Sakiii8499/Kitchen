Kaju Katli

Ingredients

Cashewnut powder 2 cups, Sugar 1 cup, Milk 1 tablespoon, Liquid glucose 1 tablespoon, Ghee 1 teaspoon to grease, Silver varq to garnish

Method

  1. Heat a non-stick pan, add sugar and Â½ cup water and bring to a boil. Add milk, mix and cook till sugar melts. Discard the scum that gathers at the sides and cook till the mixture reaches multi-string consistency.
  2. Add liquid glucose and ghee and mix well. Add cashewnut powder and mix till well incorporated.
  3. Transfer into a bowl. Grease your palms with some ghee and knead the cashewnut mixture till it resembles dough.
  4. Grease an aluminium barfi tray with some ghee. Put a portion of mixture and put into it and spread evenly with your hand. Further level the surface with a rolling pin. Trim the excess from the edges.
  5. Cover the top with silver varq and set aside to cool for 30 minutes.
  6. Cut into diamonds and serve.
