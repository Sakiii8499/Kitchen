Vanilla Ice-cream

Ingredients

1 litre milk, 1/2 tablespoon corn flour, 1 cup fresh cream, 1 cup sugar, 2 teaspoon vanilla essence

Method

  1. To make Vanilla Ice cream at home, take a small bowl and add 1 teaspoon cornflour in 4 tablespoon milk, stir it into a smooth paste.
  2. In the meantime, take a vessel and boil the milk, keep stirring so that the milk does not stick to the bottom.
  3. Reduce the milk to half. Now, add the sugar and allow the milk to reduce. Once the consistency of milk thickens, reduce the flame to low.
  4. Next, add the fresh cream and vanilla essence and mix well. After mixing, turn off the flame and pour the mixture into an aluminium container.
  5. Cover the container with an aluminium foil and freeze for 6 hours or till semi-set.
  6. Remove from the freezer and pour the mixture into a blender and blend till smooth.
  7. Cover with an aluminium foil and freeze for 10 hours or till set. Your Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream is ready!
